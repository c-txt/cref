## Jumping Hours Display

#watches #clocks

Austrian engineer Josef Pallweber is widely credited for having patented the jumping hours mechanism in pocket watches in 1883

Instead of the gradual sweeping motion of two hands on the dial, watches with jumping hours have numerals ‘jump’ to the next digit at the end of the 59th minute.

Examples: 
- A. Lange & Söhne’s Zeitwerk
- Chronoswiss Delphis
- Gerald Charles Maestro 4.0 Ducati 30° Anniversario 916
